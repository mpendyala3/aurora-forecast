About us

Built for people chasing the northern lights.

Aurora Hunt is a clean, mobile-first aurora dashboard designed to make planning simple: live space weather, local visibility, webcams, maps, and a few practical tools in one place.

What we do

We turn aurora data into something easier to understand, so you can decide quickly whether tonight is worth the drive.

Why it exists

Most aurora tools are cluttered or split across too many screens. Aurora Hunt keeps the useful parts together and readable.

Principles

  • Fast to scan on mobile
  • Useful even when data feeds fail
  • No account required
  • Privacy-first by default

Built for aurora hunters who want something cleaner than a cluttered app.
