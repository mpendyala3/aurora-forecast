Privacy policy

We keep personal data exposure low.

Aurora Hunt is designed to work with minimal storage and no account sign-in. This page explains what we keep and why.

Local storage

We may store preferences such as theme, location settings, watch thresholds, and saved watchlist entries in your browser so the site feels faster.

Submitted information

Contact, alert, and operator forms may be sent to Formspree when you submit them.

What we do not do

  • No account is required to browse the site
  • No personal email is shown on the site
  • No tracking-heavy profile system is used
Third-party embeds or services, if enabled, may have their own privacy policies.

Built for aurora hunters who want something cleaner than a cluttered app.
