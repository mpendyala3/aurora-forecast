Cookies

Minimal storage, not a cookie maze.

Aurora Hunt uses small amounts of browser storage for convenience. We do not run a heavy cookie banner or ad stack.

What we store

  • Theme preference
  • Saved location and watch settings
  • Saved watchlist entries

Why it’s stored

  • So the site remembers your choices
  • So forms are faster next time
  • So the experience feels less repetitive
You can choose essential-only mode or allow preferences in the privacy banner, and we only save optional settings when you opt in.
If we add analytics or embedded third-party services later, we’ll document the change here.
