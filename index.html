    What the numbers mean

    Kp index

    A measure of geomagnetic activity. Higher Kp means the aurora oval can move farther south.

    Bz

    When Bz turns negative, the solar wind couples better with Earth’s magnetic field.

    Cloud cover

    The sky can be perfect aurora weather and still stay hidden if clouds block the view.

    Dark sky window

    The best chance is usually after sunset and before sunrise, away from city lights.
